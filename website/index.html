Features

Why choose MrRSS?

Designed for simplicity and power, giving you control over your news feed with modern tools.

Auto-Translation

Instantly translate article titles and content using Google Translate or DeepL API. Break down language barriers and access global content effortlessly.

Localization

Fully localized interface supporting English and Chinese (简体中文). Switch languages instantly to match your preference.

Cross-Platform

Built with Wails and Go, MrRSS runs natively on Windows, macOS, and Linux. Enjoy a consistent experience across all your devices.

Privacy First

Your data belongs to you. MrRSS uses a local SQLite database to store your feeds and articles. No tracking, no external servers.
